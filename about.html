About

Outpost's Story

Outpost began in 1970 during a time when big grocery chains were at the center of grocery shopping, causing local small businesses to lose business and eventually shut down. At the same time, harmful agricultural chemicals were destroying the rural landscape and poisoning food. Concerned people in Milwaukee were looking for a way to purchase foods they considered wholesome and whole. These people pooled their interest and their money into a cooperative business structure and in April of 1970 and started the East Kane Street Food Co-op. Since that point, Outpost has changed and expanded its locations, continuing to deliver fresh, organic and local foods to the Milwaukee area.

Values

  1. Expertise
    Outpost has done extensive research on the sources of its foods and educates the public.
  2. Knowledge
    The company and staff are educated on food sourcing and food types.
  3. Sustainability
    All products comply with environmental and ethics standards.
  4. Integrity
    The company sources the highest quality products without compromising ethics.

Mission Statement

Outpost Natural Foods exists so that our owners have a healthy, diverse, and sustainable community. We will accomplish this while maintaining the long-term strength of the cooperative.
