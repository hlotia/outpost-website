About the Designer

Designer

Husaina is a designer from Milwaukee, Wisconsin. She has created this website for a rebrand project at UW-Milwaukee, where she was asked to rebrand and reimagine a local Milwaukee business. This website or project is not done in affiliation with the real Outpost Natural Foods store, and this is not their official website.
