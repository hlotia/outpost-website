Outpost is a Milwaukee-based grocery chain and Co-op. We have extensive knowledge of sustainable, ethical food. Research is at the core of our stores, as we discover new ways we can improve our company while teaching customers about where their food comes from, how to eat healthy, how to prepare the foods we sell and how to support fair trade and other ethically sourced goods. Our stores carry a variety of local products and produce, including a wide range of fruits, vegetables, bakery items and more.
